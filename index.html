Sorry Not Sorry

We all have that friend, family or future ex partner that we just can't commit to. You know the one, the one you text after a few drinks, thinking to yourself "they aren't that bad". But in reality they are that bad, and you've already committed your Friday night! You can't just use the same old "Sorry, I have to work late" because first of all, you aren't sorry and secondly you don't even work. Use "Sorry Not Sorry" to send that perfect snarky text to make sure you never have to bail again.
